Spyro the Dragon animation

Made by Raiden H for TCSS491 @ UWT

Spyro the Dragon sprites ripped from The Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning by Heartlessdragoon. Thank you!

This page is made up of 5 animations from the full spritesheet. Some problems I had to solve in the development included modifying the Animator class to loop over specific ranges of a spritesheet instead of the whole series of frames (specifically for the flying animation), as well as chaining multiple animations together (for jump + land) and toggling animations (run & fly).

My spritesheet (modified from The Legend of Spyro) can be seen here, and my background (which I drew) can be seen here. The source code for this project is available here.
